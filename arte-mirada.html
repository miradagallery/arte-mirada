The practice

Painting is the act
of sustained attention

Based in Milan, I work with oil and acrylic to investigate the space between intention and accident — the marks that arrive before thought, the silence that gathers around them.

I am drawn to the tension between field and gesture, to monochrome interrupted, to light as it behaves in industrial spaces. Each canvas is completed when there is nothing left to remove.

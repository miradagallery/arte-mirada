The practice

Painting is the act
of sustained attention

Mirada Gallery was born from a belief that art is a bridge between the visible and the felt, between what we see and what lives quietly beneath the surface.

Each piece is an original work, painted by hand in Italy using oils and mixed media. Inspired by classical references, surrealism, and the quiet magic of everyday emotion. Every painting ships directly from my studio in Milan, carefully packaged and ready to become part of your space.
